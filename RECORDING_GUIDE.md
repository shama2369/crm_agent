### **REASONS FOR NOT PURCHASING**
- "Price was the reason" → reason_price: Yes
- "Size was the reason" → reason_size: Yes
- "Weight was the reason" → reason_weight: Yes
- "Zero making charge was the reason" → reason_zeromaking: Yes
- **⚠️ IMPORTANT - Design Unavailability:** DO NOT just say "unavailable" or "item unavailable"
  - **Design Out of Stock:** "Design out of stock: Yes" → reason_design_outofstock: Yes (known/existing design not available)
  - **Design New:** "Customer requested new/custom design" → reason_design_new: Yes (new design not in catalog)
